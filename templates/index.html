Frequently Asked Questions

We decided to answer some of the most frequently asked questions to make sure you have all the information you need.

1. How does the online learning process work?

Our online learning process is flexible and self-paced, allowing you to access course materials, participate in interactive lessons, and complete assignments on your own schedule. You'll receive real-time supervision and support from our experienced instructors throughout the course.

2. What are trust proof offline exams?

Trustproof offline exams are final assessments that take place at official campuses in a secure, proctored environment. These exams ensure that your certification is credible and recognized by educational institutions and employers.

3. How long does it take to complete the course?

The duration of the course varies depending on your pace of study. On average, most students complete the course within 6 to 12 months, but you have the flexibility to progress faster or take additional time as needed.

4. Is there any support available if I struggle with the course material?

Absolutely! Our instructors are available for real-time supervision and guidance. You can reach out for help through our online platform, where we offer one-on-one support, group discussions, and additional resources to ensure you understand the material.

5. What makes Elite School different from other education centers?

With over 10 years of experience and more than 2,500 successful students, Elite School stands out for its modern teaching methods, flexible learning options, and the trusted offline exams that validate your achievements. We also have a strong reputation for credibility and support from both students and parents.
